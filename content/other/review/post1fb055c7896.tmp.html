Like many academics I do a lot of reviewing for academic journals, publishers, and funding bodies. I like to slightly pointlessly measure things and keep track of them,1 and I also think its useful to share this sort of information, because no one really knows how much of this sort of things other people do. I have no idea if I do more or less reviewing that other academics. In any case, here is a brief record of my peer reviewing.

I reviewed my first paper for a journal in 2012, and since then I have reviewed 142 submissions for 46 different journals.
